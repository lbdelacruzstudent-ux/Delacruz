Verse Today

Jeremiah 29:11 in the King James Version (KJV) is a popular Bible verse that reads: "For I know the thoughts that I think toward you, saith the LORD, thoughts of peace, and not of evil, to give you an expected end". It is commonly understood as a promise of God’s good intentions, hope, and a positive future for His people, even during times of trial.
